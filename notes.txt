## Concrete difference in scoring quality:

Right now if you rank a Mechanical Engineer and a PMO Analyst using the same prompt, the LLM has no context about what Sulzer specifically expects from each. With taxonomy routing:

1. A PMO role automatically checks for RAID logs, transition playbooks, governance frameworks
2. An Engineering role automatically checks for CAD, FEA, specific domain certifications
3. A Finance role automatically checks for ERP, financial modelling, audit experience

Even if the JD doesn't explicitly mention these — the taxonomy fills the gap.
